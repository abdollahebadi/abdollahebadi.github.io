Get to know me...!

Since beginning my journey as a freelance designer 12 years ago, I've done remote work for agencies, consulted for startups, and collaborated with talented people to create digital products for both business and consumer use. I'm quietly confident, naturally curious, and perpetually working on improving my skills. Since 2007, I started my journey as an engineer. Despite my personal preference, I found myself studying Computer Engineering, which I have to say I did not connect with initially. But things took another turn when I started to code X86 assembly. All of a sudden, I felt a click, and from then onwards, I spent day and night learning how to MOV variables from one register to another. I even wrote a snake and ladder game in Assembly. I've always had a passion for mechanical engineering, and thus I decided to major in an interdisciplinary field such as robotics to not only learn about the mechanics of life but also make use of the software/hardware knowledge that I had learned. Fast-forward to this date, and I can see the benefit of this decision. It has helped me work in a variety of fields and collaborate with awesome entities.
